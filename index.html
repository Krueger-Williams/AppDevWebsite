ESTUDIAR!

Welcome to ESTUDIAR!--a multiple-choice based quiz application allowing you to easily and quickly study your Spanish vocabulary. With an intuitive interface, multiple levels of difficulty, and hundreds of words to study from, you are guaranteed to remember and recall your Spanish vocabulary next time you may need it. Features:

  • Intuitive design
  • Vocabulary for beginners
  • Different types of grammar
  • New vocabulary being updated frequently
  • Based off of the Spanish 1 curriculum
